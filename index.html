01 Pay via QR — Scaling In-Store Digital Payments ∨
01 It All Started When...

FairPrice Group served over 2 million customers across its retail stores — but had no way to digitally engage, grow, or monetise them. These customers transacted in-store and walked away, leaving no digital footprint. The opportunity was to bring them onto the FPG App by making their everyday retail transactions seamless, embedding loyalty directly into the payment experience. The goal wasn't just a payment feature — it was to build a digitally connected customer base at scale.

02 Aditi's Role
Owned end-to-end product strategy and UX redesign of the POS-integrated QR payment flow on the FPG App.
Collaborated with engineering, retail ops, and POS vendors to ensure seamless integration across hundreds of store touchpoints.
Drove iterative experimentation on the payment UX to reduce friction and increase adoption.
03 Results
1.8 million customers digitally connected to FairPrice Group
1 million weekly active users on the FPG App — an engaged base to grow and monetise
~29% of all in-store transactions via QR, ~90% retention, CSAT 4.6
02 Building a Growth & Engagement Engine — Vouchers, Gamification & Personalisation ∨
01 It All Started When...

FairPrice Group had a growing digital customer base but was relying on blunt, discount-led promotions that generated low loyalty and poor retention. Vouchers were fragmented — customers missed eligible offers or dropped off between claim and apply. And there was no scalable engagement mechanic to keep customers coming back. The opportunity was to build a smarter, more connected growth engine: evolving vouchers into an intelligent conversion platform and layering in gamification to make the FPG digital experience genuinely engaging and habit-forming.

02 Aditi's Role
Led end-to-end redesign of the voucher platform — improving discovery, personalisation, claim, apply and redemption flows across all digital touchpoints to reduce drop-off and increase conversion.
Launched and scaled a personalised gamification platform supporting engagement mechanics, reward journeys, and promotional personalisation for customers across the FPG App.
Drove iterative A/B experimentation across both voucher and gamification journeys, partnering with data and marketing teams to build personalisation logic and measure ROI on promotional spend.
03 Results
15% growth in user acquisition and 10% uplift in retention via optimised voucher journeys
300K+ customers engaged on the gamification platform, delivering 2.6x ROI on promotional spend
20% increase in WAU — an engaged base ready to grow and monetise
Vouchers evolved from passive discounts into a scalable, measurable conversion engine
03 Checkout CRO — ~$3M GMV Recovery ∨
01 It All Started When...

FairPrice Group's digital checkout was losing significant revenue to payment failures and drop-offs. 02 Aditi's Role

Coordinated with payment gateway partners, engineering, and QA to systematically eliminate failure categories.
Ran A/B experiments on checkout flows, error messaging, and retry logic to improve recovery rates.
03 Results
45% reduction in payment failures across digital checkout
~$3M GMV uplift from recovered transactions

06 Building BNPL Products for India's Unbanked ∨
01 It All Started When...

Home Credit India was on a mission to bring financial inclusion to the unbanked and underbanked population in India's offline market. Traditional credit products were inaccessible to new-to-credit consumers, and existing collections and engagement models were inefficient. The challenge was to design BNPL products that were simple enough for first-time credit users, profitable at scale, and supported by strong retention and collections infrastructure — all across a three-pillar strategy: new revenue channels, consumer and merchant loyalty, and optimised cash flow.

02 Aditi's Role
Owned end-to-end product strategy for a portfolio of BNPL products — including a revolving EMI card for new-to-credit users, co-lending partnerships with banks, and telecom postpaid BNPL bundles.
Built a customer rewards engine and led a consumer app revamp (Aadhaar-based KYC, improved ledger, chatbot) to deepen engagement and reduce service costs.
Integrated payment gateways for instalment collection to streamline cash flow and reduce delayed payments across the portfolio.
03 Results
50K users onboarded on revolving EMI card in 3 months, ~60% utilisation rate
₹170 net revenue per transaction via co-lending partnerships, ~13% risk reduction
7% increase in MAU and 4% reduction in customer service calls from app revamp
~10% improvement in cash flow efficiency via payment gateway integration
